Here are some fun origami tutorials

These are easy to learn and show simple steps to create your own origami masterpieces.

Origami Crane

  • The crane is one of the simplest origami creations.
  • It is also a very popular choice for many origami enthusiasts.

Origami Heart

  • This origami heart is even simpler than the crane, but just as fun to attempt!
  • It is also a great option for a handmade present for a loved one.

Origami Sailboat

  • This origami sailboat may not hold in water, but is certainly fun to make and have some fun with.
  • It is perhaps the most popular origami craft, and quite simple for a beginner.

Origami Dinosaur

  • This origami dinosaur is great for dinosaur enthusiasts and voracious crafters alike.
  • It is tougher than the other crafts so far, but what's the fun if there's no challenge?
